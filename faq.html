Getting Started

Creating an account is simple and free! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation, fill in your basic information including name, email, and password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account. Once verified, you can start posting ads immediately.

Yes! We offer a free Basic plan that allows you to post up to 3 ads per month. For users who need to post more frequently, we have Professional and Business plans with additional features and benefits. Check our Pricing page for detailed information about all plans.

You can sell almost anything legal! Popular categories include vehicles, electronics, furniture, clothing, books, services, and real estate. However, we prohibit the sale of illegal items, weapons, adult content, counterfeit goods, and any items that violate local laws or our terms of service.

Posting an ad is easy! Click the "Post Ad" button, choose your category, fill in the details about your item or service, upload photos, set your price, and provide contact information. Your ad will be reviewed and typically published within 24 hours. Make sure to provide accurate descriptions and clear photos for better results.

Account & Security

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link is valid for 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely and we never share your information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law. We also offer two-factor authentication for added security.

Yes, you can change your username once every 30 days. Go to your account settings, click on "Edit Profile," and enter your new username. Note that your old username will become available for others to use, so choose wisely!

We're sorry to see you go! To delete your account, go to Account Settings and click on "Delete Account." Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. Your active ads will be removed, and your data will be deleted in accordance with our privacy policy.

Buying & Selling

Look for verified seller badges, read reviews from other buyers, check their response rate, and review their posting history. Always meet in safe, public places for transactions, and never share financial information before meeting in person. Trust your instincts - if something seems too good to be true, it probably is.

We recommend cash for in-person transactions, or using secure payment platforms like PayPal for online deals. Avoid wire transfers, gift cards, or unusual payment methods. Never pay in full before receiving the item, and always get a receipt for your transaction.

If you encounter a suspicious ad or user, click the "Report" button on the ad page or user profile. You can also email our support team at support@a-services.com with details. We take all reports seriously and investigate them promptly to maintain a safe marketplace.

First, try to resolve the issue directly with the other party through our messaging system. If that doesn't work, contact our support team for mediation. We can help facilitate communication and, if necessary, take appropriate action based on our terms of service.

Pricing & Payments

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners. We also offer invoicing for business accounts.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with your subscription, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Add-on features and featured listings are non-refundable once published.

Free ad allowances do not roll over to the next month. However, any purchased add-ons like featured listings can be used whenever you want within your subscription period. We recommend using your full allowance each month to get the most value from your plan.

No hidden fees! The price you see is the price you pay. We're transparent about all costs. The only additional charges are for optional add-ons that you specifically choose to purchase, like featuring your ad or marking it as urgent.

Technical Support

Ads typically go live within 24 hours after submission. If your ad isn't showing, it might be under review, or there could be an issue with the content. Check your email for any notifications from us. Make sure your ad complies with our guidelines and doesn't contain prohibited content.

When posting an ad, click the "Upload Images" button and select photos from your device. You can upload up to 10 images depending on your plan. For best results, use clear, well-lit photos that show the item from multiple angles. Supported formats include JPG, PNG, and GIF, with a maximum file size of 5MB per image.

Yes! Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store. The app allows you to browse, post, and manage ads on the go, with all the features of the desktop version optimized for mobile use.

You can reach our support team through multiple channels: email at support@a-services.com, phone at +1 (555) 123-4567 during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM EST), or through our live chat feature available on the website. Premium plan members get priority support with faster response times.
